CRAFT BIZ — Logo & Brand Assets
=================================
Place your logo and brand image files here.

  hamna.png            → Owner / team photo (used for About section)
  logo.png             → Main logo (transparent background, PNG)
  logo-white.png       → White version of logo (for dark backgrounds)
  logo-dark.png        → Dark version of logo (for light backgrounds)
  favicon.ico          → Browser tab icon (also place in project root)

SPECS
-----
• Logo PNG:  at least 400 × 400 px · transparent background
• Favicon:   32 × 32 px and 16 × 16 px combined .ico
• Photo:     800 × 800 px minimum · good lighting

HOW TO USE IN INDEX.HTML
-------------------------
To add a logo to the navbar:
  <img src="images/logo/logo.png" alt="Craft Biz" style="height:32px">

To add to the footer or About section:
  <img src="images/logo/logo-white.png" alt="Craft Biz Logo">
