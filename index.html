Help

Frequently Asked Questions

What file formats do you accept?▾
We accept DXF, SVG, AI, PDF, PNG, JPG, STL, and STEP files. For laser cutting, DXF and SVG give the best results. If you only have a photo or rough sketch, share it on WhatsApp and we'll convert it for you at no extra charge.
What is the minimum order quantity?▾
Most products have a minimum of 1 piece. Keychains and stickers have a minimum of 5–50 pieces. PVC ID cards minimum is 50 cards. We do single-piece custom orders for prototypes and samples — just WhatsApp us.
How long does production take?▾
Simple laser-cut items: 1–2 days. T-shirt printing: 1–2 days. 3D printing: 2–4 days depending on model size. Complex CNC or large batch orders: 3–7 days. Wedding card sets: 5–10 days. We will give you an exact timeline when you place the order.
Do you ship across India?▾
Yes. We ship to all states via reputable couriers. Free shipping on orders above ₹999. Delivery takes 3–7 business days depending on your location. Local Calicut customers can also pick up from our studio.
What acrylic colours are available?▾
We stock clear, milky white, black, red, blue, green, yellow, frosted, gold mirror, silver mirror, and glitter acrylic. Thickness range: 2mm to 10mm. For a specific colour you don't see here, WhatsApp us — we can source it.
Can I get a sample before placing a bulk order?▾
Yes. We offer sample pieces at regular price before you commit to a bulk order. WhatsApp us your design and we will produce one sample first. Many corporate clients do this before ordering 100+ pieces.
What payment methods do you accept?▾
We accept UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), NEFT/bank transfer, and cash on delivery for local orders. Online payment details are shared after order confirmation. 50% advance is required for custom orders above ₹1000.
